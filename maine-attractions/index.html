Maine Attractions

During peak clamming seasons on the coast of Old Orchard Beach, my dad would take my siblings and me to the beach to look for the ones that escaped the nets. We would find clams bigger than our two hands and fry them up in flour and lots of butter. Our family friends, the Minors, own property and houses right on the shore, so when my dad went up to work on roofs and siding and other contracting handywork, we all got to play on the beach. When Denise Minor asked me to do some work preparing the houses for the summer, I jumped at the chance to spend six weeks in a place that was more consistent to me than any other house in my life. So, it's going to be short and probably uneventful, but here's my life on the beach!
