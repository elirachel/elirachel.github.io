London-ing

London, baby. It's where it's all at. I'm now including myself in "it all". London is where I'm at, and I'm here all year! I'm studying at the Institute of Education with University College London, getting my Master's of Arts in Sociology of Education. If you're thinking about studying in London, I'll be adding a section with some posts and insights into student life soon. I'll also be keeping track of my reflections on the contents of my courses, so look out for that. But for now London-ing is the day-to-day adventures, the extraordinary and the mundane. All the mundanity is extraordinary when you're living in London.
