Portland Days of a Portland Month

I'm spending the month of April in Portland, Oregon, where cyclists and pedestrians are first-class citizens. I'm keeping track of the things I learn about this place from the people I meet and the opportunities I embrace. Apparently, donuts, beer, and coffee are Portlanders' three main food groups, and every successful restaurant here caters to all dietary preferences/restrictions. Although I can't bring myself to consume more than half a cup o' joe every six months, I imagine I'll fit in well enough in the City of Roses.
